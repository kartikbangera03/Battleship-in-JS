BATTLESHIP
Battleship is a two player guessing game. The left board is yours and the right board is your opponents, in this case your computers. Each game board is made of 10x10 grids and five ships. Ships have already been secretly arranged for both players. Each turn consists of guessing the location of a ship on their opponents board. Your Goal is to sink all of your opponent's ships by correctly guessing their locations before your opponent sinks all of yours. The winner is the first player to sink their opponent's ships before all of their own are sunk. Types of ship i) Carries - 5 squares ii) Battleship - 4 squares iii) Cruiser - 3 squares iv) Submarine - 3 squares v) Destroyer - 2 squares.
